Key Findings
Finding 01
Faithfulness — LLaMA 3 Wins
LLaMA 3 achieves a perfect faithfulness score (1.0) meaning zero hallucination detected across all 25 compliance questions. For regulatory contexts where auditability is mandatory, this is the most critical metric.
Finding 02
Speed–Quality Tradeoff Is Real
LLaMA 3 is 13× slower than DistilBERT (12.2s vs 0.9s) but scores 47% higher on RAGAS. For a compliance tool used by professionals — not a real-time chatbot — this tradeoff favours quality over speed.
Finding 03
Retrieval is Model-Independent
Context Precision and Recall are identical across all three models (same ChromaDB retriever). This proves the retrieval pipeline is the shared bottleneck — improving chunking or re-ranking would benefit all models equally.
